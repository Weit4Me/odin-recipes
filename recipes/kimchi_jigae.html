Kimchi Jigae

Description

Kimchi jigae is a Korean stew made with kimchi, pork, tofu, and other ingredients. Itâ€™s a popular dish in Korea and is often served with rice. Kimchi jigae is known for its spicy, sour, and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups kimchi, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound pork belly, sliced
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 block tofu, cubed
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 red chili, sliced (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add kimchi, gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, and sugar. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Add tofu and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with green onion and red chili, if desired. Serve hot with rice.
